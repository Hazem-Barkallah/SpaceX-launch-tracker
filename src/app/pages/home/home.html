About SpaceX

SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

Pioneering the Future

SpaceX has gained worldwide attention for a series of historic milestones. It is the only private company capable of returning a spacecraft from low-Earth orbit, and in 2012 our Dragon spacecraft became the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to and from the International Space Station.

We are developing the technologies to make life multiplanetary. SpaceX is building a network of next-generation satellites to bring fast, reliable internet to underserved areas of the globe.

Our ultimate goal is to make humanity a multi-planetary species by establishing a self-sustaining city on Mars.
